## Application layer.
Responsible for expose end-points and handle requests to guide to another services.
Logs can be placed here, because is easy to handle the returns on try catch, and its useful to log there.

## Service Layer
Responsible for handle business logic and validations.

## Domain Layer
Responsible for implementation of classes and models that will map the database.
Also has DTO, VO, enums, etc.

##Infrastructure
Divided by two sub layers:
	- Data : Will CRUD database, may use a ORM.
	- CrossCutting : CrossDomainLayer methods, they can be used anywhere.
